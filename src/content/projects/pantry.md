# Overview

I keep buying cilantro and forgetting. The plan was: a tiny menu bar dropdown with what's in the fridge, and what's about to die. I never intended to ship it. I just wanted the thing.

## The shortcut

The fun part was the keyboard shortcut: command-P pops a quick-add. Tab, type, enter. It made me feel like the kind of person who has a clean kitchen.

## Shelved, lovingly

Stopped using it because we moved apartments and my new fridge is tiny enough to see everything in. The repo is still there. Sometimes I open the file just to look at the spacing.
